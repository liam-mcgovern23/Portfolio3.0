About Me

I am a dedicated and driven student specializing in Data Analytics, currently pursuing a Bachelor of Business Studies with a concentration on various different platforms, tools and skills at Dublin City University. My academic journey has been complemented by hands-on experience in various analytical roles, including a pivotal position as a Fraud Analyst. I honed my skills in data manipulation and analysis using advanced tools such as Python, SQL, and Power BI throughout various modules in college such as Professional Business Analytics Portfolio, Data Mining & Predictive Analytics and Workflow and Data Management. My career ambition is to harness my analytical acumen and technical prowess to significantly influence business strategies and operational efficiency in a leading-edge corporate setting. With a strong commitment to professional growth and a passion for leveraging data to solve complex business challenges, I aim to continuously expand my skill set and knowledge base through ongoing education and real-world applications. I envision myself contributing to and leading projects that utilise big data to drive innovation, optimise processes, and improve decision-making. My ultimate career objective is to ascend to a leadership role within the technology and analytics sector, where I can shape industry standards and practices while mentoring the next generation of data scientists and analysts. This trajectory is not only a professional goal but also a personal commitment to excel in a field that combines my interests in technology, business, and problem-solving. By aligning with organisations that value innovation and data-driven decision making, I strive to make a meaningful impact on the business landscape and achieve excellence in my future endeavors.

This tutorial would help beginners understand the basics of SQL, enabling them to retrieve and analyze data effectively.

Each one has its own special place in my week, helping me grow as a person and enjoy life to the fullest.
